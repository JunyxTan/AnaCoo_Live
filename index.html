Good to know

Questions, answered

How long do alterations take?
Most basic alterations take 1–3 working days. More complex adjustments, curtains or custom bedsheets may need a little longer. We confirm the estimated completion date when you send your item in.
Where are you located in R&F?
We are home-based at Block A7-1 — not located inside the shopping mall. Parking is available at the building.
Do you offer rush service?
Yes — rush service may be available for simple alterations depending on our schedule. Please contact us early and we’ll let you know the earliest possible completion time.
Can a zip replacement be done in one day?
No. We don’t keep zips in stock due to the wide variety of colours, sizes and lengths, so a little time is needed to source the right one.
Can you help source fabric?
We don’t provide fabric sourcing, but you’re welcome to bring your own fabric for repairs, curtain work or custom bedsheets.
Do you require a deposit?
For most basic alterations, no deposit is required. A small deposit may be requested for custom bedsheet making or larger projects.
Are there limitations to alterations?
Some garments have design or fabric limitations that affect what can be done. We always assess your item and advise the best possible solution before starting.
